# Setup for the first Mission

Shuffle the Skirmish Deployment cards of all available heroes and draw 8 cards at random. These are the starting heroes. The player then chooses 3 of those heroes to field in the first mission of the campaign.  
Each hero gains \+2 speed to their default speed. Each hero has a new starting weapon, depending on their normal starting weapon.

* Melee starting weapon: [[New Items and Starting Weapons#Plasteel Staff|Plasteel Staff]]
* Ranged non-pistol starting weapon: [[New Items and Starting Weapons#A280 Carbine|A280 Carbine]]
* Ranged pistol starting weapon: [[New Items and Starting Weapons#Glie-44|Glie-44]]

Additionally, each hero can be equipped with 1 accessory of the [[New Items and Starting Weapons#Starting|starting accessories list]]. 
The starting Mission is “Aftermath” (Core Campaign p.4). Jump to the [[Story Missions#1 Aftermath (Core Campaign p.4)|appropriate section in the Story Mission changes list]], for the changes to this mission.
# Campaign Setup (after the initial mission)

* Shuffle all available green and grey side missions into the Side Mission deck.

# Campaign Round Steps

* Add 2 damage per face down [Medkit](./New%20Items%20and%20Starting%20Weapons.md) to a hero. Then remove facedown Medkits.  
* Draw new Side Missions  
* Healing  
  * Each hero with damage rolls 3 yellow dice. Remove 2 damage for each Surge rolled.  
* Facility functions  
  * Dedicate each hero to a facility (if able)  
  * Roll the appropriate dice  
  * Resolve any results  
* Resolve special actions of facilities  
* Spend credits to buy any unlocked items, facilities, upgrades, troops, …  
* Advance the Campaign Timer  
* Pick a new mission.  
* If the picked mission is a Side Mission, discard all other available Side Missions.  
* Pick heroes up to the shuttle capacity to join the mission.  
  * Heroes with wounds still on them can not be chosen for the squad.

# Facilities and space on the Cruiser

## Free rooms for facilities

The [Gozanti-class cruiser](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Gozanti-class_cruiser) has a working engineering station, a research lab, and 6 unused rooms that the player can build additional facilities in. Some facilities may be required to advance the campaign story missions. Facilities (only if they are not mandatory for campaign progress) can be torn down to free up rooms. Tearing down a room does not cost resources or time but also doesn’t refund spent resources during construction of the facility.

Rooms are located on 2 decks in sets of 3, directly over each other. Some facilities might benefit from being adjacent to certain other facilities.  
Rooms on the upper deck can be cleared quicker than rooms on the lower deck.

## Standard Facilities

### Engineering

* Buy starting and unlocked items.  
* Build new facilities:  
  * Dedicated heroes roll their Tech test. Add resulting surges to the progress of the current facility. Facilities can be bought without putting progress on them. Progress can be split across multiple facilities.  
  * If a facility is completed in this step, special actions from facilities can be resolved in the same campaign round. “Overflow progress” can be added to a new facility, provided the player has enough money to buy it.

### Research

* Conduct research.  
  * Dedicated heroes roll their Insight test. Add resulting surges to the research progress of any available project. Progress cannot be split across multiple projects.  
  * If a research project is completed in this step, the resulting buy options can be resolved in the same campaign round. “Overflow progress” can be added to a new project.

## Buildable Facilities

These facilities can be built into one (or more) of the 
### Lab
* \+2 Surges per dedicated troop member for research.  
* Unlocks Specialized Training research.  
* Upgrade: Additional \+2 Surges per dedicated troop member for research.
Cost: 200 Credits
### Workshop
* \+1 Surge per dedicated troop member for building facilities or droids (droids are mechanized troops that do not get wound penalties and will be introduced with the [[README#Reworked Characters and Abilities ~Q4/26 - Rebel Reinforcements|Rebel Reinforcements]] Update).  
* Unlocks Special Weapons research.  
* Unlocks Upgrades for other facilities.
Cost: 200 Credits
### Training Facility
* Unlocks Research for Skills that cost 4XP.  
* Train troop: Dedicate a hero (becomes unavailable for the following 2 missions) to gain 1XP. The hero cannot be dedicated to other tasks during that time.  
* Upgrade: \+2 slots for training troops.  
* Buy  
  * Physical conditioning  
    * \+1 health per XP skill a hero earned  
  * Boom  
    * \+1 damage with grenades for heroes equipped with the [[New Items and Starting Weapons#Under Barrel Launcher|Under Barrel Launcher]] attachment.  
  * Ping  
    * \+1 surge and \+2 accuracy when using a weapon with the “Long Rifle” trait  
  * Dead Shot  
    * \+1 damage and \+1 Surge when attacking with a weapon with the “Gun” trait while adjacent to the target space  
  * Woosh:  
    * \+1 Evade when wearing the [[New Items and Starting Weapons#Combat Coat|Combat Coat]].
Cost: 250 Credits
### Infirmary
* Infirmary bed: Dedicate troops (max. 2\) to double the amount healed. Dedicated heroes are unavailable for the next mission.  
* Upgrade: Bacta Tank. Choose a hero. Reduce the amount of damage on that hero by 10\. Limit once per Campaign Round.  
* Upgrade: More beds. Increase the troops that can be dedicated here to a maximum of 6\.  
* Upgrade: Another Bacta Tank. Choose up to 2 heroes.
Cost: 200 Credits
### Comms station
* After revealing Side Missions, draw 3 Side Missions. Replace one revealed Mission with one drawn mission. Shuffle the remaining missions back into the deck.  
* Buy  
  * Troops: Spend 80 credits to draw another hero from the deck and add them to your roster.  
  * Intel: Spend 40 credits to reduce the ODS timer by 1\.
Cost: 130 Credits
